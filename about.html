About NoteFlow

NoteFlow was born from a simple idea: studying should be organized, focused, and less stressful. As students ourselves, we juggled countless apps ‚Äì one for notes, another for to-do lists, separate timers, and flashcard apps. It was chaotic and inefficient.

NoteFlow is built with care, focusing on a clean interface, robust features, and the promise of a one-time purchase for lifetime value. We're committed to continuously improving NoteFlow and supporting our community of learners.
